ABOUT

DT Thorne is an indie electronic and hip-hop music producer from Boise, Idaho. He has been instrumentally trained his whole life through piano, has played many other instruments such as the drums, guitar, ukulele, and trumpet, and has performed in bands. He has made music for video games and has worked in game design. DT continues to sharpen his craft and live his passion for producing.

When he's not in the studio, DT enjoys playing soccer and other sports such as basketball and bowling. He enjoys photography and capturing the unseen aspects of the world, as well as graphic design and creating album art.
