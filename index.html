ABOUT

DT Thorne is an indie electronic producer from Boise, Idaho. He has been instrumentally trained his whole life through piano, has played many other instruments such as the drums, guitar, ukulele, and trumpet, and has performed in bands. He has made music for video games and has worked in game design.
