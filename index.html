Hey! I'm DT, an aspiring music producer.

About

Music has always had a strong effect on my life. Since early childhood I've been playing the piano and my passion for music has only gotten stronger. In 6th and 7th grade, I learned trumpet and played in the middle school jazz band. Later, I took interest in playing drum set and took lessons, while self teaching ukulele and guitar. During that time, I was influenced by my brother to start composing music and explored creating rhythms and melodies.

Making music to me is more than just writing notes and beats. It connects to me emotionally and can be what gets me through the day when times are hard. Even when I'm not in the studio composing, I find myself constantly singing a song in my head or drumming on tables. It's become a part of who I am as a person.

I find going on walks and listening to music helps me get inspired to write. I'm always eager to grow and learn, and to find what will make me a better artist and individual. I want to share my experiences and feelings with people, and that's what keeps me motivated.

Other Interests

I've always played soccer and enjoy playing other sports such as basketball and bowling. Skiing and snowboarding are also some things I've done. I've found that working out really helps my mental focus as well as my physical being.

I've learned how to code in many different languages such as BASIC, Java, and Python, and have taken interest in programming and designing video games. When I began creating games in BASIC, I made music along with the code.

Photography is a hobby that has grown recently along with graphic design. I enjoy capturing the unseen aspects of the world and designing album art.
